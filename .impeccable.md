# Design Context

## Users
Luxury homeowners ($500K+ homes) seeking premium electrical services. They invest in high-end projects: luxury chandeliers, smart home systems, EV charger installations, comprehensive renovations. They value craftsmanship, expertise, and attention to detail. They browse on phones and tablets at home, often comparing multiple providers.

## Brand Personality
**Voice:** Confident & Bold & Sophisticated
- **Three words:** Technical precision, premium craftsmanship, refined authority
- **Emotional goals:** Trust (licensed, insured), confidence (expert execution), sophistication (elevated taste)
- **Differentiation:** We're not just electrical workers - we're partners in home transformation

## Aesthetic Direction
**Visual tone:** Technical sophistication meets refined luxury
- **Reference:** Tesla's website - clean, premium, confident, innovative, premium tech feel. Black with metallic/gold accents, bold typography, generous white space, precision photography
- **Anti-references:** Generic electrician sites, busy layouts, stock photo vibes, "handyman" aesthetic, cheap contractor vibes, cluttered information
- **Theme:** Dark mode as primary (premium, sophisticated, works with Tesla reference), with light mode for accessibility
- **Colors:** Black + Gold is correct, but needs refinement. Deeper blacks (not #000), warmer gold (more 18K than 24K), subtle metallic gradients, strategic accent use (10% rule)

## Design Principles

1. **Precision in every pixel** - Typography, spacing, alignment must feel intentionally crafted
2. **Visual rhythm, not repetition** - Varied spacing, asymmetric layouts, thoughtful composition breaks
3. **Quality over quantity** - Fewer, better images. No stock photos. Professional photography that shows craftsmanship
4. **Trust through clarity** - Clear hierarchy, direct communication, no marketing fluff, transparency builds confidence
5. **Performance is premium** - Fast loads, smooth animations, immediate feedback - luxury users expect speed
